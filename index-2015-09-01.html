The ADX clan

The ADX clan was created on 31 July 2015. We are a group of casual players who love the Contract Wars game. Most of our progress is documented by the leader cwaddict who is recording all his gameplay sessions, also doing them on live stream, and uploading them to youtube.

We did a sky-rocket start, we were climbing up the latter from position 30,000 to top 700 within 21 days.

cwaddict is the first person who live-streams Contract Wars for the community.
"Nice to be the first, let's play!" ;-)
